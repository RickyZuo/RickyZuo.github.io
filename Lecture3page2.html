Facts about pugs:

Pugs belong to the companion classification of dogs. Pugs are one of the oldest dog breeds around. Pugs are an incredibly distinct breed in terms of appearance. Pugs also have a classic underbite, which gives them a comical, faux-grumpy expression. Although they’re small, Pugs can be dependable watchdogs.With their gentle, playful personalities and sturdy bodies, Pugs make great canine companions for families with small children. They were considered to be good luck because their forehead wrinkles resembled the Chinese character for “prince.”
